# Publication Decision for RIVERS-CORE-0001

RIVERS-CORE-0001 is approved for publication as the canonical historical conception record of the RIVERS protocol.

The record has completed the following process:

* Independent human conception draft
* Independent AI technical draft
* Adversarial external peer review
* Human–AI consideration of the review findings
* Follow-up publication-readiness assessment
* Canonical draft synthesis
* Final external review
* Human founder approval

The published record is intentionally historical and non-normative. It represents the best justified understanding available during the original conception of RIVERS.

Later discoveries—including Question as the generative unit, Understanding as a distinct stage, the relationship between the protocol record and the Atomic Unit of Knowledge, and the possible evolution from RIVERS to QUIVERS—are intentionally not backported into CORE-0001.

Those developments shall be preserved through subsequent CORE records that cite, challenge, refine, or supersede the original conception.

CORE-0001 is therefore accepted not as a finished protocol specification, but as the traceable origin from which the protocol will continue to evolve.

**Decision:** Approved for publication
**Published Status:** Historical Conception
**Normative Status:** Non-normative
**Supersedable:** Yes
