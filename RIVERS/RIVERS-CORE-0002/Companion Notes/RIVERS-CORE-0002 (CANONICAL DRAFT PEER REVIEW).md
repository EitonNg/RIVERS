## 11. Remaining gap to normative transition

Benefits of Question and Understanding do not yet justify normative transition because the following remain incomplete:

- Semantic definition (identity/promotion)
- Governance design (triage authority, retention, privacy)
- Practical pilot evidence
- Comparative evidence
- Authority and adoption procedure

**All are necessary for full supersession.** A narrower bar could justify experimental or recommended use earlier; it should not justify full replacement of RIVERS yet.

---

## 12. Evidence threshold for transition

Proposed minimum evidence ladder:

| Level | Minimum evidence |
|-------|------------------|
| Experimental use | Several worked examples + independent review of those examples |
| Recommended use | Cross-domain pilots + failure-mode analysis of triage/capture + user feedback |
| Provisional normative use | Controlled RIVERS-versus-candidate comparison + retrieval/reuse testing + explicit authority model for promotion/standardization + formal CORE approval |
| Full supersession | Provisional normative use sustained over time + migration/governance rules + public supersession record that leaves CORE-0001 historically intact |
